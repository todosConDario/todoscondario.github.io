Darío's Story


In 2012 my dad, Dario Arzaba, was diagnosed with a rare blood disease called myelodysplastic syndrome. This past January the disease entered an acute phase, and treatments became more aggressive. As a result of these aggressive treatments, he has experienced multiple life-threatening infections that required weeks of hospitalization.

Through it all, my dad has kept a positive attitude. He continued painting, making friends with his doctors and nurses, and being a light for his family and friends. He has never given up on the dream of returning to his normal life, of completing his dream architectural project, and of spending time with his family at home.

Historia de Darío


En 2012, a mi papá Dario le diagnosticaron una rara enfermedad de la sangre llamada síndrome mielodisplásico. El pasado mes de enero la enfermedad entró en una fase aguda y los tratamientos se volvieron más agresivos. Como resultado, ha experimentado múltiples infecciones que requirieron semanas de hospitalización.

A pesar de todo, mi papá ha mantenido una actitud positiva, ya que continúa pintando, haciéndose amigo del personal de salud y siendo una luz para su familia y amigos. Nunca ha renunciado al sueño de volver a su vida normal, de completar el proyecto arquitectónico de sus sueños y de pasar tiempo con su familia en casa.
